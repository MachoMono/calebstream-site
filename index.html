CalebStillStreams Pipeline
calebstream.fyi

CalebStillStreams Pipeline is a personal automation tool that connects Caleb's Twitch channel to his YouTube channel. When a live stream ends on Twitch, the pipeline automatically updates the corresponding YouTube VOD with a custom thumbnail, SEO-optimized description, tags, and chapter markers.

CalebStillStreams Pipeline

How It Works

  1. 1Twitch sends a stream-offline webhook when the broadcast ends.
  2. 2The pipeline polls YouTube until the VOD is fully processed.
  3. 3It extracts the best frame from the VOD for use as a thumbnail.
  4. 4A custom thumbnail is generated and uploaded to YouTube.
  5. 5Title, description, tags, and chapters are applied automatically.

This tool accesses only the YouTube account belonging to CalebStillStreams and is operated solely by Caleb for his own content. No user data is collected or shared with third parties.
